Control Access

The different ways to control access to e-resources are Username and password. Some of it's advantages are:-

  • Access to some e-resources is controlled by username and password.
  • This facilitates usage analysis, minimizes unauthorized access and ensures compliance with license agreements.
  • All e-resources that are available off-campus are controlled in this way.
  • Details of usernames and passwords can be obtained from any Library Enquiry Desk.
