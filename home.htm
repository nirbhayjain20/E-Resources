Introduction

E-resources is short term for Electronic Resources or electronic information resources. These are collections of information in electronic or digital format that are accessed on an electronic device, such as a mobile phone, computer, etc. These are published resources in electronic versions/format such as encyclopedias, pamphlets, e-books and e-journals. Our e-Resources provide access to literally thousands of magazines and newspapers far more than the library could possibly subscribe to in paper format. E- resources is distribution of information in any electronic form such as CD- ROM, Floppy Disk or Magnetic tape or across a computer network like e-journals, E-Books, ETD etc. accessible dial-up bulletin board or on-line services.

Purpose

Main purpose of electronic resources is providing current information.

Up date information is necessary for research work.

Through electronic resources users are able to access latest information.

Save the time of user and staff
